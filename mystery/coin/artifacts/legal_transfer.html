JUSTIFICATION FOR TRANSFER

Pursuant to California Health and Safety Code § 7114.5 (Hazardous Biological Materials Handling) and California Health and Safety Code § 102850 (Infectious Disease Control), and in accordance with Fresno County Contract #FC-2019-0847 (Specialized Preservation Services), the remains of Case #2024-08-0847 require immediate transfer to a certified hazardous materials facility.

Contamination Documentation

Previous contamination report filed by Medical Examiner (S. Martinez, MD) documents presence of hazardous isotopic material requiring Level 4 containment protocols. County facility does not possess adequate cryogenic preservation capability for long-term storage of contaminated biological specimens.

Regulatory Compliance:

  • California Code of Regulations, Title 17, § 30253 (Hazardous Materials Storage)
  • 24-hour statutory deadline for hazmat transfer decision per HSC § 7114.5(c)(3)
  • Radiation Safety protocols require immediate specialized containment
